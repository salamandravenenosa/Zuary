# Zuary product marketing context

## Product
Zuary is a marketing analytics dashboard for agencies that manage dental clinics and local service businesses.

It pulls marketing data from Instagram, TikTok, Google Analytics, Google Business Profile, Meta Pixel, and future WhatsApp sources. The product helps the agency show clients what happened, what improved, and what needs action.

## Audience
- Primary buyer: marketing agency owner or operator.
- Daily users: agency team, account managers, clinic owners.
- Customer type: local businesses that do not want raw analytics tools.

## Positioning
Zuary is not a generic analytics dashboard. It is a client-facing proof layer for marketing work.

The main promise:
Show results clearly enough that a client understands the value without opening GA4, Meta, TikTok, or Google Business Profile.

## Voice
Use direct Portuguese. Short sentences. No hype.

Good:
- "Os posts aparecem na próxima coleta."
- "Faltam eventos de lead ou agendamento."
- "Veja o que cresceu, o que engajou e o que precisa de atenção."

Avoid:
- "cockpit"
- "transforme seus dados"
- "desbloqueie insights"
- "experiência poderosa"
- "jornada rumo à excelência"
- "em tempo real" unless the data is actually live
- long explanations inside UI

## Visual copy rules
- Headings should be short.
- Tooltips explain one thing only.
- Empty states should say what is missing and what to do next.
- Status labels should be plain: "Atualizado", "Sem dados", "Não conectado".
- Do not over-explain metrics in cards.

## Design direction
Dark product UI with purple as the main identity color.

Use platform colors only as small signals. The app should look like Zuary first, not Instagram, Google, or TikTok.

## Useful marketing ideas from marketing-ideas
- Customer Language: use the client’s real words from agency conversations.
- Public Demos: show real product walkthroughs for sales and onboarding.
- Template Marketing: offer client report templates or monthly review templates.
- Calculator Marketing: create a free ROI/report value calculator for agencies.
- Founder Welcome Email and Onboarding Emails: help new agency users connect integrations and invite clients.
- Comparison Pages: position Zuary against spreadsheets, Looker Studio, and manual PDF reports.
